Anyway, the second (or middle) part of this expedition, journey, shenanigan, waste of time, call it whatever you will, we will be exploring something that is definitely a step more casual than the previous restauarant, Le Gemelle, and it is a burger joint! I know what you might be thinking "How could one of the "grandest restauarants of Amman" be some measly burger joint?!", and you kind of have a point. But actually, you don't, sure fancy restauarants are meant to be the best restaurants that a city has to offer, but just think about it, why would you go to a fancy restaurant every single day and essentially TORTURE your wallet? Wouldn't you sometimes want to eat something more casual, like a burger? Don't we all crave McDonalds at 3 AM for no reason? There, point proven. However, McDonalds is everywhere, and isn't really special, but Burgerrizz is arguably higher in quality than most chicken/burger joints I've seen here, and trust me, I've seen it all in Amman, so it indeed deserves a spot here. Since this is a fast-food joint, this restaruant has many branches, but not many since it isn't a multi-million dollar food chain, but enough to statisfy a good area of regions in Amman. Anyway, enough with the wordy context! Let's munch and crunch away!

As mentioned before, Burgerrizz is a burger fast-food joint, having many joints around Amman. Burgerrizz serves both Meat and Chicken burgerz, also serving an optional side of chicken tenders. This restaurant excels in both the Meat and Chicken aspects, but me and my family found that the chicken is higher in quality than the beef, but the meat nontheless is still excellent. What makes this restaurant special though is the high quality of sandwiches, where it almost sometimes doesn't feel like a fast food joint, in which the chicken and meat are girthy and juicy, and the toppings on the sanwiches creatively add flavor to the food. Additionally, the menu is indeed diverse, featuring burgers with unique toppings, such as the Machine Burger that has Mozzarella sticks inside it, the Code Burger that has swiss and cheddar cheese AND fried onion rings, the Hash Crunch that has Rocca and a Hash brown, the Hot Z that has Jalapeno and Chili Cheese, and so much more! Luckily, if you like keepin' it simple, Burgerrizz has a standard Bugerrizz classic featuring simple ingredients with no surprises. However, out of all this diversity, I naturally have prefences that I would indeed recommend you trying. I have included below a list of the three items I recommend and a detailed breakdown of them, which will include two chicken and one meat burger to make all the chicken enthusiasts and meat lovers happy! Here are my recommendations:

1. Hot Z Chicken Burger! (9.5/10)
2. Crunchy Chicken Burger (9/10)
3. Rocca Burger (8.5/10)

Burgerrizz has many good options in terms of chicken and meat burgers, but these three burgers in particular stood out, the chicken burgers in particular are done in a way that I personally perfer, in which they are fried rather than grilled, allowing the chicken to have a nice crunch and more juice and girth, and in particular the Hot Z has an incredible flavor with the Jalapenos adding a nice kick of spice, and the chili cheese adding more spice with more flavor along with the swiss cheese creating a spicy, and cheesey burger, and the crunchy chicken being nice and simple but still fantastic with a special crunch. The meat burger mentioned in particular has a unique flavor with the ingredients incorporated within the burger, more specifically the rocca adding a subtle background flavor that enhances the more prominent ingredients such as caramelized onions that add a good crunch and a strong flavor, and the white mushroom sauce being magnificent, giving the burger a nice, saucy texture along with the strong, but very much welcoming mushroom flavor, and all of these work together to form an amazing burger!
