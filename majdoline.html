Majdoline

Arab Cuisine Perfected... The Best of the Best...

9.5/10!

Did ya notice the extra content on this page? Trust me, this restaurant is an absolute beaut' mate. Anyway, welcome to the third and last part of this expedition, journey, shenanigan, rubbish, waste of time we will be exploring another rather fancy restaurant, but this time it is one that aligns with Jordan's cuisine more closely, Lebanese! Lebanese cuisine features many classic Arabian and Mediterranean dishes, and Majdoline indeed features many of those dishes, such as Kibbeh, Kuftah, Kebab, and even Shawerma! The variations of these dishes that Majdoline has, believe me, do NOT disappoint, they indeed capture Lebanese and Arab cuisine in an artful and flavorful way that I subjectively believe no other place does! The setting of the place also creates a soothing and fancy atmosphere that makes you feel like a king, and the presentation of the food makes it even more appetizing and therefore allows you to enjoy the food and restaurant even more! Once you see the menu, you may or may not gawk at the astonishing prices, but I assure you it is worth at least trying once! Anyway, enough with the blabbing, let's end this tour off with a BANG!

Kebab & Shawerma!
Majdoline Menu

Majdoline as mentioned is a restaurant with an Arabian/Mediterranean cuisine (or lebanese), it also is located around the center of Amman, where it will be to most people who live/visit Amman, and it has many dishes from that cuisine, such as Kebab in the form of a Mixed Grill, featuring kebab, and meat and chicken cubes, Kubeh being Fried Kubeh, being minced meat covered in a blend of cracked wheat, and shawerma which has been reformed to be unique and flavorful being Shawerma Cake! All these dishes mentioned are most certainly worth trying, but unfortunately I have only been able to experience this restaurant once, and I was not able to sample much of the food that they have to offer, but amongst the food I was able to sample I have some favorites that are worth mentioning and I believe you should indeed try eating! However, remember that I am a fifteen year old at the end of the day, so I may not critique these magnificent foods accurately, and my taste is not a golden stamp per se, so take my recommendations with a grain of salt. Nontheless, here are my three dishes that I found to be my favorites, this time around, there are two dishes and one desert!
