In the first part of this expedition/shenanigan whatever you will, we will be exploring a gem in the middle of this salty ocean of Jordan, and that is Le Gemelle. I know what you might be predicting, "Sounds French to me, oui oui baguette!" Well firstly, that is a stererotype, secondly, the restaurant is actually Italian![1] You may or may not have inferenced that from the images shown on the previous page and on this one, but that's besides the point. This restaurant although hefty in price is definitely worth trying at least once. The appetizers indeed get your tastebuds fired up and the Pastas and Pizzas there are explosive in flavor. The restaurant is located rather on the outscirts of Amman and not near where most people live, being located in Dabouq, which is a small part of Amman where most of the wealthy like his Majesty[2] lives. Anyway, I don't want to continue boring you with context, let's take a look at this beauty!

Le Gemelle Menu

As mentioned before, Le Gemelle is an Italian restaurant that is located on the outscirts of Amman, therefore there the food palette consists mainly of Pastas, Risottos, and the fan favorite Pizza! The menu features a unique array of these foods that give the menu diversity. From the pastas include Spaghetto Bolognese, Seafood Pasta, Penne, Ravioli, Vegetarian and Beef Lasagnas, and much more! From the risottos include Seafood Risotto, Mushroom Risotto, Pesto Risotto, and more! Finally, from the pizzas include Margherita, Rocca, Pepporoni, and more! Despite this diversity, I subjectively have favorites as like all humans I have a unique set of tatsebuds. However, take these favorites that I have with a grain of salt, as I did not try much of the food at Le Gemelle, so my judgement is not 100% credible. Nontheless, I will give you the three dishes I tried at the restaurant that I found best and rank them in order! Below is the menu as a reference, and below that are my rankings:

1. Ravioli (w/creamy white sauce)(9/10)
2. Seafood Pasta (8/10)
3. Rocca Pizza (7.5/10)

These dishes in particular had the most explosive flavor, if you will. They were extremely well done and exceeded my teenage food standards, and the flavor of these dishes are what make Le Gemelle a special restaurant that is on my top 5 grandest restaurants of Amman! For instance, the Sea Pasta had a unique flavor with the use of sea food such as shrimp, some of which added texture to the dish that made it feel special. Additionally, a sauce added a nice soft feel to the pasta and a vivid flavor. With the white sauce ravioli, the ravioli itself was spectacular, with the spinach and chesse combining together in your mouth to create an awesome flavor bundle, and the ravioli having the right texture, not being too chewy, but being just right, and the white sauce was just out of this world, being creamy and cheesy, and bundling with the ravioli to create an indeed explosive and organized flavor inside your mouth. And finally, the Rocca was a great pizza, the chesse and tomato sauce was not overwhelming but rather felt subtle, allowing for it to be the foundation for the flavorful rocca that combines with the parmesan to create a solid pizza!

In short, Le Gemelle is a fantastic restaurant. The atmosphere of the restaurant is organized and neat, paired with the fantastic food makes for a terrific restauarant. The food is not too heavy, but rather just right, which makes it close to perfection. However, relative to the other restaurants in this tour, it does not even come close to their perfection. So for this reason, it is at the bottom of the top 3, but nontheless is still a great place to eat at it. With all that in mind, Le Gemelle earns a solid 8/10 overall.
